About Game
Kenshin is an online open world action MMORPG that is set on Nibiru, Nibiru is themed after the Edo period of japan. Nibiru is divided into 4 region which is Akimitsu, Himari, Asakusa and Saeko. Player will pick one of the available character and explore the world of Nibiru with the choosen character. In Nibiru players will interact with other player, samurai themed npcs and defeat monsters spread throughout the world.
FEATURES
RAID
Raid mode is a team based activity, where players can team up with their friends or matchmake with other players online, Raid is a challenging activity riddled with tough enemies and puzzles. to succeed players must team up with other player to defeat the enemies and solve the puzzles
EXPLORATION
Kenshin is an open world game thus player are free to explore the world of Nibiru to grow their character and understand the world of Nibiru. Player will also uncover mysteries through puzzles found in the wild, these mysteries will help you understand the world better
TIME ATTACK
In time attack mode players are tested to clear a difficult mission within a set period of time. Players mastery of their characters movement, skill and strategy are tested
